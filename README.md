# Red-Mountain-Makers-project
Our mobile app project for Red Mountain Makers, a NFP organization of greater Birmingham.
Red Mountain Makers is Birmingham metro area's non-profit Maker community. Their Mission is to provide a collaborative
environment that promotes discovery, creativity, and exploration of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Mathematics).
Their facility is located at 5502 1st Avenue North and acts as a hub for all of their activities.
